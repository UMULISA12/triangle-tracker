TRIANGLE TRACKER

This tracker will check whether the entered values can make an equilateral Triangle or isosceles triangle or scalene, it will also show if the entered values can not make a triangle.




Isosceles:
Two sides are exactly equal.

Equilateral:
All sides must be equal.

Scalene:
None of the sides is equal.
However, the sum of any two sides of
the triangle must be greater
than the third side.

Not a triangle:
Most importantly, if the sum of any
two sides of the triangle is equal to
or less than the third remaining side,
then a triangle CANNOT be
formed using those values.
